About the Festival

The East/West International Piano Festival invites you to join us for its inaugural season in the beautiful seaside resort city of Weihai, China, one of the easternmost cities of China. Embracing China’s extraordinarily scenic views and rich cultural heritage, this two-week festival promises to be an unforgettable, once-in-a-lifetime experience. The Festival is open to outstanding pianists from throughout the world. Each participnts will receive at least 5 full private lessons from requested teacher, coaching for piano ensemble assignments in addition to 5 master classes instruction from extraordinary international artist teachers. There will also be opportunities for all participants to perform in public concerts at the conclusion of the Festival. In addition, the artist faculty will participate in special concerts given for the participants.

Application Deadline: May 15, 2016

ALSO, enjoy an excursion to the Easternmost peninsula of China!
