Tuition

2016 East/West International Piano Festival Tuition:$2,200 USD

Tuition and Fees includes:
* 6 full individual lessons with requested teacher
* 6 master classes with other faculty members
* Participation in recitals and concerts
* Participation in piano literature presentations
* Free attendance to recitals and concerts
* 2 weeks room and board
* Free access to all the practice facilities
* Transportation from airport to hotel
* One day excursion to Qingdao

The tuition for students who do not request to live at the hotel will be $300 less ($1900).
Charges for each parent who may wish to stay with their child will be $500 for the duration of the festival (1 parent and 1 child per room），which will also include the meals and lodging expense.

A parent or guardian must accompany any students accepted under the age of 18 years.
students are responsible for making their own individual travel arrangements to/from the Festival.

*The Festival will not be responsible for individual medical expenses.

  • Deadline

    Application Deadline: May 15, 2016 (including CD/DVD/link and $100 non-refundable application fee)

    Tuition Deposit due 7 days after notification of acceptance to the Festival: $400 USD

    Balance of Tuition due ON or BEFORE June 1, 2016 ($2200 USD less $400 USD deposit= $1800 USD)
